DYSTOPIAN

The year is 2050… and the world has fallen silent. In the ashes of failed governments and broken systems, a new force has appeared the Dominion. They promised a new order. They promised peace. And we believed them. But their peace came with a price… control. The Dominion controls everything. They built the Humannet: a network that links every human to their system. Through it, they watch. Through it, they listen. And through it, they command. Your thoughts… are not your own. Your emotions… are not your own. They decide what you feel, what you think, what you become. Most people don’t even realize they’re slaves. They wake, they work, they sleep, all while the Dominion pulls the strings from the shadows. But there are a few—those who remember what it means to be free. They fight, in secret, against an enemy that knows their every move. They are the rebels, a small band of outcasts who refuse to let their minds be shackled. In dark corners, they gather, sharing whispers of a past where choice was alive. But every act of defiance is met with brutal punishment, as the Dominion’s enforcers relentlessly hunt them down Because in a world where the Dominion controls the mind, rebellion is a crime punishable by erasure. Step out of line, and they don’t just take your life… they take you

About the Author

Moustapha was born in Algeria and raised in London, where he discovered his love for storytelling. His unique background helps him create powerful stories about human freedom, survival, and the fight against controlling powers. Moustapha has written several books that have sold over 500,000 copies around the world.

Key Characters

Cassia: A former officer turned rebel, determined to fight the Dominion’s control.
The Dominion: The all-powerful force, shaping minds and crushing rebellion.
